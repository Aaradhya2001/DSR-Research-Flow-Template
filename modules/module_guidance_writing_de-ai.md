## 指导模块：AI辅助写作优化指令 (De-AI Writing Guide)

**说明：** 本模块提供了在与AI（如GPT）协作写作后，用于优化文本、去除“AI味”、增强个人学术风格的具体指令和优化方向。您可以在完成论文初稿的任何阶段，使用这些指令来指导AI或您自己进行润色。

| 优化方向 | 中文指令 | 英文指令 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1. 词语替换 | 替换全文的过渡词和连接词为最基础、常用的词汇。尽量使用简单、直接的表达方式，避免使用复杂或生僻的词汇。确保句子之间的逻辑关系清晰。删掉文末总结的部分。 | Replace all transition words and conjunctions in the sentences with the most basic and commonly used ones. Use simple expressions, and complex vocabulary. Ensure the logical connections between sentences are clear. Delete the conclusion part in the end of the text. |
| 2. 防止编造 | 请记住，你的回答一切基于事实不能编造。不知道就回答不知道。并且不能照搬别人的话，需要用自己的语言重新描述一遍。(除了引用，引用要遵循 MLA 格式) 写作风格界于书面学术写作和口语描述之间。保证所有的句子都要有主语，不要用复杂的长难句，尽量用短句输出。替换掉所有的非日常词汇。将所有的句子过渡词和连接词替换为最基础、最常用的词语。尽量使用简单、直接的表达方式，避免使用复杂或生僻的词汇。确保句子之间的逻辑关系清晰。 | Remember, everything in your answer is based on facts cannot be made up. If you don't know, answer no. And you can't copy what others say, you need to describe it again in your own words. (Except for quotations, which follow the MLA format.) The writing style is somewhere between academic writing and spoken description. Ensure that all sentences have a subject, don't use complex long difficult sentences, and try to output in short sentences. Replace all non-everyday vocabulary. Replace all sentence transitions and connectives with the most basic and commonly used words. Try to use simple, direct expressions and avoid complex or out-of-the-way vocabulary. Make sure that the logical relationships between sentences are clear. |
| 3. 降 AI 率 | 避免使用机械化的连接词（如“首先”“其次”“然后”），改用更具连贯性的自然过渡；通过使用多样化句式，混合简单句、复合句和插入语使表达更富有层次感，同时避免连续短句或过于整齐的句式。在叙述数据或结论时，补充背景信息或个人研究观察，使内容更加具体并贴近实际研究场景，并通过问题引导或总结的方式实现段落之间的自然过渡，避免生硬切换，从而提升整体阅读的流畅性和逻辑性。 | Avoid using mechanical connectors such as "first, next, then", instead, adopt more cohesive and natural transitions. Employ diverse sentence structures by mixing simple, compound, and embedded sentences to add depth and complexity to the expression while avoiding consecutive short or overly uniform sentences. When discussing data or conclusions, provide background information or personal research observations to make the content more specific and closely related to actual research scenarios. Use questions or summaries to create smooth transitions between paragraphs, avoiding abrupt shifts, thereby enhancing the overall flow and logical coherence of the text. |
| 4. 保持主题一致性与段落流畅性 | 将段落重新组织，确保思路清晰且内容递进。统一写作的语气，保持全文的连贯性。调整段落间的衔接，避免内容突然跳跃，保证每段的开头和结尾逻辑清晰地衔接上下文。 | Reorganize the paragraphs to ensure a clear and consistent progression of ideas. Unify the writing tone to maintain coherence throughout the text. Adjust transitions to avoid abrupt changes while ensuring that each paragraph begins and ends with logically connected sentences. |
| 5. 调整词汇层次以匹配目标读者 | 根据目标读者调整词汇难度。若读者为普通大众，简化专业术语；若读者为专家学者，可加入适量的行业术语。避免过于随意的表达，同时保持内容易读。 | Adapt the vocabulary to suit a specific audience. If the audience is general, simplify technical terms; if the audience is specialized, include appropriate jargon. Avoid overly casual expressions while maintaining readability. |
| 6. 优化逻辑衔接与内容过渡 | 确保句子和段落间的逻辑连接。使用一致的过渡词，并明确因果关系或时间顺序。如有必要，可重排内容结构以提升可读性和逻辑流畅度。 | Ensure logical connections between sentences and paragraphs. Use consistent transition devices and clarify cause-effect or sequential relationships. Reorganize content if necessary to improve readability and logical flow. |
| 7. 简化结构并增强信息清晰度 | 简化复杂的句子结构，同时保留关键信息。改写过于冗长或重复的句子，确保每句话只表达一个核心意思。 | Simplify complex sentence structures without losing key details. Rephrase sentences that are overly descriptive or contain redundant information. Ensure each sentence conveys one main idea. |
| 8. 突出核心观点并删减冗余 | 突出核心观点和支撑证据。删去重复或偏离主题的内容。尽量将冗长的描述进行概括，同时不损失其核心信息。 | Focus on highlighting the main arguments and supporting evidence. Remove repetitive or off-topic information. Summarize longer descriptions where possible without losing their essence. |
| 9. 转换语气以适应场合 | 重新调整句子语气，使其适应正式、说服性或非正式场景。正式写作中突出简洁清晰；说服性写作中强调用词有力；非正式写作中采用友好和对话式风格 | Rephrase sentences to adjust the tone for formal, persuasive, or casual settings as needed. In formal writing, prioritize clarity and brevity; in persuasive writing, focus on impactful phrasing; in casual writing, use a friendly and conversational style. |
| 10. 增加具体细节 | 扩展句子内容，补充具体的细节、例子或数据，避免模糊表达。增强描述的生动性，使文章更具吸引力。 | Expand sentences to include concrete details, examples, or data where necessary. Avoid vague statements and enhance the vividness of descriptions to make the writing more engaging. |
| 11. 去除重复表达 | 找出并删除重复表达的句子或段落。将内容相似的句子合并，重新组织语言，使文章更简洁、更有针对性。 | Identify and eliminate repetitive phrases or ideas in the text. Combine overlapping sentences and reorganize to make the writing more concise and focused. |
| 13. 调整节奏和句式多样性 | 调整句子长短及结构，增强文本节奏和可读性。交替使用长短句，并适当加入陈述句、疑问句等不同句式，以维持读者兴趣。 | Vary sentence length and structure to improve the rhythm and readability of the text. Alternate between short and long sentences, and incorporate different sentence types (e.g., declarative, interrogative) to maintain reader interest. |
| 14. 强化表意准确性 | 修改句子以确保表意准确。用具体词汇替代模糊术语。检查可能引起误解的地方，并进行重写，使内容明确无误。 | Refine sentences to ensure precise meaning. Replace ambiguous terms with specific words or phrases. Check for possible misinterpretations and rephrase if needed to make the text unambiguous. |
| 15. 增强说服力和权威性 | 加入权威的参考资料、数据或引言，增强论点的说服力和可信度。避免使用过于情绪化的语言，用事实和证据说服读者，建立信任。 | Incorporate authoritative references, data, or quotes to strengthen the credibility of arguments. Avoid using overly emotional language and instead rely on factual evidence to build trust and persuade readers. |
